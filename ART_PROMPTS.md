# Art Prompts — Your New Best Friend

All prompts share a consistent style anchor. Prefix every prompt with this **style block**, then append the creature/item-specific block.

---

## Style Anchor (prepend to ALL prompts)

> **Anime-influenced digital concept art, painterly brushwork with soft cel-shading and smooth color gradients. Dark moody environment with strong neon rim lighting in cyan, magenta, and red. Bokeh depth-of-field blur on the background. The subject is primarily an organic living creature with subtle cybernetic augmentations — NOT a robot or mech, the animal should look alive and mostly biological with small technological additions visible under or on the skin. Slightly stylized proportions, expressive eyes, polished render quality. Color palette: desaturated dark backgrounds with high-saturation neon accent lighting. Soft atmospheric glow and light bloom on cybernetic elements. No text, no watermark, no UI elements.**

---

## ACTOR PORTRAITS (square, 512x512 or larger)

Append to style block. These are character portraits with dark vignette backgrounds. The creature must look like a **real living animal first** — warm, organic, with real fur/skin/scales — that happens to have some cybernetic parts. Think "a pet that got augmented", not "a robot shaped like an animal".

---

### 1. Datarabbit — Portrait

> Small rabbit, three-quarter angle, compact crouching pose. Soft white-grey fur, natural rabbit proportions, alert upright ears. One eye is a glowing cybernetic implant with a pink-red iris lens, the other is a natural dark eye. Sleek chrome and dark plating along the back and haunches fitted over the fur like lightweight armor segments — not covering the whole body. Ears have faint circuit-trace patterns visible under translucent skin at the tips. One forepaw has a small glowing fingernail — a homing tracer.

---

### 2. Forever Turtle — Portrait

> Large, unsettling mutant turtle, front-facing. Leathery reptile skin, wrinkled and ancient — an unnatural amalgamation of multiple tortoise species with mismatched shell plates and uneven scute patterns. Shell has a subtle dark metallic sheen from subdermal armor underneath the living tissue. Jaw is slightly oversized with faint chrome reinforcement along the jawline, teeth just a bit too sharp and metallic. Eyes are small, old, and coldly intelligent with a faint red inner glow. Thin green bio-luminescent veins pulse along the neck. Warty, scarred skin.

---

### 3. Neon Newt — Portrait

> Small, pocket-sized newt, three-quarter angle. Moist delicate skin, natural newt proportions, slender body. Skin shifts between vivid neon pink, electric blue, and toxic green in an unnatural bioluminescent gradient, glowing softly from within. Tiny glistening acid droplets bead on the skin surface. Mouth slightly open with a faint warm orange glow deep in the throat. Eyes are large, round, glossy black, and expressive. No cybernetic hardware visible — all modifications are biological. Slim limbs, small toes, delicate amphibian anatomy.

---

### 4. Obsidian Ocelot — Portrait

> Sleek, elegant ocelot, regal pose. Jet-black fur with a faint obsidian shimmer, natural feline musculature and grace. One eye has a faint amber targeting reticle overlaid on the natural pupil. Patches of fur along one flank shimmer and refract light slightly as if dissolving into the background — a subtle chameleon coating effect. Jaw is natural cat-shaped but slightly reinforced with thin chrome lines along the jawline, closed. Claws faintly glint metallic at the tips. Subdermal armor shows only as barely-visible geometric lines under the fur on the shoulders.

---

### 5. Cyberbear — Portrait

> Massive grizzly bear rearing up on hind legs, head thrown back roaring upward, both arms raised and spread wide in a dominance display. Dramatic low angle, viewed from far below looking up. Thick brown-black fur, heavy musculature. Unnaturally huge with grafted muscle visible as ridged bulges under taut skin on the shoulders and forearms. Jaws stretched wide roaring at the sky — teeth are just a bit too metallic and sharp, subtle chrome reinforcement. A small neural link port glows faintly blue at the base of the skull, partially hidden by fur. The skull is subtly misshapen — asymmetrical, slightly swollen on one side above the brow, with patchy thin fur and darkened, veiny skin where the bone bulges outward from the tumors growing inside. Eyes are bloodshot, pupils unevenly dilated, gaze feral. Deep old scars across the muzzle and brow. Chrome-tipped claws on both forepaws spread wide, catching the light. Subdermal armor visible only as a faint dark geometric pattern through thin fur on the chest.

---

### 6. Cyberboa — Portrait

> Large snake, coiled. Glistening scales, muscular coils, natural serpentine anatomy. Scale patterns are subtly wrong — cobra hood markings blending into viper diamonds into coral snake bands, as if multiple species were spliced together. A small chrome flashbulb dome on the crown of the head like a third eye — the only obvious external hardware. Mouth partially open, elongated fangs with a faint metallic sheen, a tiny green droplet on one tip. A small neural link port glows blue on the back of the head, partially hidden by scales. Eyes are slit-pupiled, reflective, coldly intelligent. Scales along the spine have a slightly harder, reinforced texture from subdermal armor underneath.

---

### 7. Cyberrat — Portrait

> Small, scrappy rat, three-quarter pose, alert posture. Dark grey-brown fur, whiskers, small pink paws, natural rodent anatomy. One eye is a small glowing red cybereye lens, the other is a natural dark bead. A tiny chrome cyberaudio bump behind one ear. Fur has a very subtle mesh-like texture from skinweave woven into the hide. Front claws and incisors have a faint green venomous glow at the tips. A tiny neural link port at the base of the skull, nearly hidden by fur. Intelligent expression, whiskers forward.

---

### 8. Cyberwolf — Portrait

> Powerful wolf, combat-ready stance. Thick dark grey-black fur, natural lupine musculature, alert ears, piercing gaze. Wearing a fitted matte black tactical vest with subtle red stitching. Jaw is natural wolf-shaped but with faint chrome reinforcement along the jawline, slightly open to show teeth that glint metallic. One eye has a faint red targeting reticle glow overlaid on the natural amber iris. A small sleek cyberaudio module behind one ear. Strong, disciplined posture.

## ITEM ICONS (square, 128x128 or 256x256, iconic object on dark background)

Use this item-specific prefix instead of the style anchor for cleaner icon results:

> **Cyberpunk item icon, single object centered on a dark transparent background, clean sharp edges, neon accent lighting, semi-realistic digital art, no text. Suitable as a small UI icon.**

---

### 1. Chipware Compartment — Icon
> A cybernetic eyeball split open to reveal a hidden compartment inside containing four tiny glowing microchips arranged in a row. The eye iris is metallic with circuit-board trace patterns. Faint cyan glow from the chip compartment. Chrome housing with a visible rotational seam where the eye spins open.

### 2. Combat Jaw — Icon
> A heavy hydraulic jaw prosthetic, chrome and matte black, viewed from a three-quarter angle. Two rows of reinforced metallic teeth, visible pneumatic pistons at the hinge joint. Red accent lights along the jaw line. Industrial and brutal-looking, clearly designed for combat. Resembles a bear trap crossed with dental prosthetics.

### 3. FBC Chameleon Coating — Icon
> A section of cybernetic body plating that is half-visible and half-dissolved into its background, demonstrating active camouflage. The visible half shows dark chrome armor plating with hexagonal texture. The cloaked half is a shimmering, refracting distortion effect with faint prismatic edges. A single glowing activation indicator light pulses blue.

### 4. Flashbulb — Icon
> A cybernetic forearm module with a large, bright lens mounted on the wrist. The lens is a polished dome emitting an intense white-yellow flash burst with radial light rays. Chrome housing with two visible option-slot connectors. Warning stripes (yellow/black) around the lens rim. The flash is blindingly bright, dominating the icon.

### 5. Hardened Cybereye Casing — Icon
> A single cybernetic eyeball with a heavily reinforced military-grade outer casing. The casing is matte dark grey with visible bolt heads and armored ridges. A faint blue EMP-shield energy field shimmers around the surface. The iris is a standard red cybereye lens visible through a small armored viewport. A tiny "MIL-SPEC" stencil marking is barely visible on the casing.

### 6. Homing Tracer Cyberfinger — Icon
> A single cybernetic finger, chrome and articulated, pointing to the right. The fingertip has a small glowing green directional indicator arrow projected holographically. The fingernail is slightly detached, revealing it as a removable tracer component with a tiny blinking red LED. Visible mechanical joints and servo details along the finger segments.

### 7. Trauma Response Nanomatrix — Icon
> A glowing internal implant capsule, oblong and translucent, filled with thousands of tiny green nanobots visibly swarming inside. Faint repair-symbol (wrench/cross hybrid) holographically projected above it. The capsule has medical-grade chrome endcaps with injection ports. A subtle green pulse wave radiates outward, suggesting armor-repair activation. Clean, medical, high-tech.
